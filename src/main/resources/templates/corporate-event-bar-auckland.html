Menus that work for corporate events

This is where people mess up. A huge cocktail list sounds impressive, but it slows service and creates queues. For corporate events, a tight menu wins, guests get served faster, and the bar stays clean.

  • 2–4 signature cocktails that match your event vibe
  • High-speed classics for broad appeal and consistent builds
  • Mocktails so non-drinkers still feel included
  • Service pacing aligned to speeches and key moments
