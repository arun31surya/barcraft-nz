FAQ

Do you supply alcohol?

No, it’s BYO alcohol. We guide you on quantities and what to buy if needed.

Do you provide glassware?

Yes. Glassware is available and can be included based on your package and guest count.

Do you only work in Auckland?

No, you can book BarCraft NZ nationwide. Auckland is the main base.

How long is a normal booking?

Most events are 3 to 5 hours service, plus setup and pack-down time.

Can you do non-alcoholic events?

Yes. We can run a premium mocktail bar for family-friendly events.

What do I need to provide?

Usually alcohol (BYO), and a small space for the bar setup. We bring tools and setup.

How far in advance should I book?

Weekends book out first. If your date matters, message early.

Can you customize a drink menu?

Yes. We can create a tight menu that works for your crowd and speeds up service.
