What actually matters

  • Type: Boston shakers seal better for speed. Cobbler shakers are beginner-friendly but can stick.
  • Seal and release: If it sticks every time, you’ll hate it.
  • Strainer quality: A bad strainer ruins everything, including your speed.
  • Finish: Matte hides fingerprints. Mirror looks premium but shows everything.

What to avoid

  • Huge “30-piece” kits full of useless junk tools
  • Ultra-thin tins that dent and leak
  • Plastic strainers
