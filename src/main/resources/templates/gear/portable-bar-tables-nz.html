Buying checklist

  • Locking legs: Non-negotiable.
  • Worktop size: You need space for ice bucket, tins, and garnish tray.
  • Height: Too low kills your back, too high feels unstable.
  • Portability: Carry handle and fold-flat design matters.

Common mistakes

  • Choosing looks over stability
  • Buying a narrow table that can’t hold tools and ice
  • Ignoring wobble on uneven floors
